20 YEARS OF SAFETY EXCELLENCE

Based in rural Oxfordshire with an exceptionally qualified team, vigilance is in our DNA.

Since 1996 our business has grown year-on-year. Our services are based on ensuring an excellent client relationship and extensive in-house expertise.

We support clients worldwide including Marketing Authorisation Holders, Contract Research Organisations, Distributors and Study Sponsors.

We are a flexible and responsive team whose aim is to ensure your budgets are respected, timescales are kept and objectives are achieved no matter the scope or scale of your project.

As a result the majority of our business comes from repeat business, referrals and personal recommendation.

We place a premium on offering challenging and rewarding careers, investing significantly in training and development

Our team includes pharmacists and science graduates who have gained postgraduate pharmacovigilance qualifications and accredited EudraVigilance training for ICSRs and XEVMPD.

Debbie Prudden
Director

Debbie is the founder and Managing Director of GoldenZone and remains at the heart of the business.

Her professional career started in hospital pharmacy and later progressed into the Pharmaceutical Industry where she had over 15 years’ of experience in Regulatory Affairs. All of which proved invaluable in her career move to work in pharmacovigilance.

It is her responsiveness to the clients’ needs and personal approach that have ensured the company has grown and evolved to provide PV services internationally.

She maintains a hands-on approach by acting as the EEA QPPV and continues to guide her team in delivering excellence to all clients on a day-to-day basis.
